About Chef Manisha Sharma

Founder of Manisha’s Kitchen

Chef Manisha Sharma is a culinary artist, food educator, and founder of the acclaimed restaurant brand Manisha’s Kitchen. With over 15 years of experience in the food industry, she is known for blending traditional Mediterranean flavors with modern presentation and techniques.

Career Highlights

  • Trained at Le Cordon Bleu, Paris
  • Opened 3 award-winning restaurants across Europe
  • Featured in “Top International Chefs” 2023 edition
  • Regular guest on culinary shows and food expos

Philosophy

Manisha believes in cooking with heart, simplicity, and seasonality. She emphasizes using fresh, local ingredients and supports sustainable farming. Her dishes tell a story — rooted in culture, crafted with precision.

Current Projects

Alongside managing her restaurants, Manisha hosts virtual cooking workshops, collaborates with food startups, and mentors aspiring chefs through her “Cook to Lead” initiative.
